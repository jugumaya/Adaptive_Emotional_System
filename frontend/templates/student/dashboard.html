Wellness resources

Non-clinical self-help tools and guides for students

🫁 Box Breathing

Inhale 4s → Hold 4s → Exhale 4s → Hold 4s. Repeat 3 times. Reduces anxiety in minutes.

Try it now in the Breathing Exercise tab!
🎯 Pomodoro Focus

25 min of focused study + 5 min break. This cycle significantly reduces cognitive fatigue.

Use apps like Forest or Focusmate to stay on track.
😴 Sleep Hygiene

7–9 hours of sleep directly improves emotional regulation, memory, and academic performance.

Avoid screens 30 minutes before bed for better rest.
🏃 Physical Activity

20 minutes of walking releases endorphins that improve mood for up to 12 hours.

📓 Journaling

Writing 5 minutes daily about your emotions builds self-awareness and reduces overthinking.

💬 Reach Out

Your university counselor provides non-clinical emotional support. You are not alone.

Contact: counselor@iub.edu.bd
